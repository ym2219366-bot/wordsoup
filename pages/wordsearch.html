Behind every great Spanish word unscrambler is a very opinionated cat with impeccable vocabulary.

El Gato says: "I did not choose the vocabulary life. The vocabulary life chose me. Also, I knocked the dictionary off the shelf at 3am. Varias veces."

Why we built Descifrador

Spanish is the second most spoken language in the world — and one of the most beautiful. Whether you're a student, a Scrabble enthusiast, a crossword devotee, or a language learner trying to crush Wordle Español, you need a tool that understands Spanish.

Most word unscramblers online are built for English. Spanish presents a different challenge: accented characters, gendered nouns, dozens of conjugated verb forms, and a vocabulary that borrows from Arabic, Italian, and indigenous languages.

And yes, El Gato is the mascot. He came with the apartment and refused to leave. He also refuses to acknowledge that Spanish has irregular verb conjugations, but we're working on that.
